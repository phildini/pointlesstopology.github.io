About Pointless Topology

Pointless Topology is a four-and-a-half bedroom apartment in the Mission, home to four-and-a-half folks who are in some way or another involved in software development and fans of free software.

The name derives from a subfield of topology that, unlike most branches of topology which study points and the lattices formed by sets of points, treats the lattices themselves as basic elements.
